Danny Barnes



Danny Barnesis 12 years old and plays Bass guitar for Meltdown Cinema (MC). Danny started as a piano player at the Piano Cottage when he was 5. After several years of piano, he decided to make a switch to Bass, an instrument he has grown to love. Danny has appeared as a member of the past 2 Piano Cottage Rocks! concerts, providing Bass support on numerous songs. MC is his ﬁrst experience in a band and he has loved the experience of making new friends with his bandmates and learning the process of rehearsing, collaborating and putting on a show. When not jamming with Meltdown, he enjoys playing soccer, basketball and listening to and learning rock music like The Red Hot Chili Peppers and Steely Dan.
